About Me

Hey there! My name is Chirag Jamadagni and I am a Computer Science Master's student at Georgia Institute of Technology. Developing new applications/systems and improving existing ones gives me a thrill and I love all the challenges associated with doing so. Some of the stuff I've worked on can be found on my Github repository and my resume can be found here.

I completed my undergrad at National Institute of Technology Karanataka, Surathkal in May 2017, and I majored in Computer Science and Engineering.

I was born and grew up in Stamford, Connecticut, and then moved to Bangalore, India for high school and undergrad. Since I am a US citizen, I don't have any visa issues and I don't require visa sponsorship to work in the US.
